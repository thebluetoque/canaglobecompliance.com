Corporate Governance Made Simple

As a public company you know that regulatory compliance and corporate governance are an unavoidable part of running your business. They’re complex and time-consuming tasks, and your investors expect you to deliver both, cost-effectively and efficiently.

Outsourcing to CanaGlobe is an economical, reliable way to ensure your compliance at all times. We offer a full range of Corporate Secretary Services as well as filing services, including SEDAR, SEDI and EDGAR.

You can trust us to provide clear answers as well as accurate, timely service.

We can help you navigate the maze of listing requirements mandated by stock exchanges and securities commissions, and take care of electronic regulatory filings in both Canada and the United States.

We assist a range of clients, from private companies through to mid-sized corporations.
