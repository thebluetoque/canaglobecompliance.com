FAQ: SEDAR (FINANCIAL STATEMENTS)

  • When do I have to file my financial statements?
  • What certificates do I use when filing financial statements?
  • Are there filing fees associated with financial statements?

ARE THERE FILING FEES ASSOCIATED WITH MY FINANCIAL STATEMENTS?

Yes. There are fees payable to securities commissions when filing audited financial statements; the total will depend on where you are a reporting issuer.

British Columbia $600 Quebec $1066
Alberta $300 Nova Scotia $283.45
Saskatchewan $250 Newfoundland $250
Manitoba $100 New Brunswick $250
Ontario * Others none

* Ontario fees are based on market cap and are calculated according to OSC Rule 13-502. To view or download the forms, please click here.

In addition, you must also pay two annual fees: the SEDAR continuous disclosure fee ($495.00 + GST) and the SEDI user fee ($1,100 + GST). These fees are payable electronically via SEDAR.

The fees noted above will differ if you are a short form prospectus issuer (44-101 issuer). Please contact us for assistance in determining these fees
