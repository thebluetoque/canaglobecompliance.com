SEDAR

Continuous disclosure filings are complex and time-consuming, and the penalty for errors or late filing can be severe. Our reliable, affordable SEDAR filing service will help you stay compliant.

Why CanaGlobe?

As SEDAR specialists, we are able to offer you an economical, efficient way to ensure your compliance:

Specialist Knowledge:

  • We have an in-depth understanding of the SEDAR system, including how and when to file, required documentation and fees.
  • Because we understand the process and keep an eye on the big picture, we can be proactive in ensuring that your filings are accurate and on time.

Better Service:

  • Our philosophy is not just to be a filing agent, but to endeavor to advise our clients of any items requiring attention or correction prior to filing.
  • We will keep you informed every step of the way, including a confirmation email once we receive your documents, followed by another once the filing is complete.

Convenience and Savings:

  • The location of your filing agent determines whether you will be subject to the Harmonized Sales Tax (HST). Because CanaGlobe Compliance Solutions is based in Alberta, we are only required to collect the 5% GST, and not the HST.
  • Our clients’ needs vary, so we offer a variety of options. You may choose our “pay as you go” filing method, or prefer to budget your annual filing costs with a pre-set annual filing package.
  • Working from Alberta, on Mountain Standard Time, we are able to offer a two hour advantage to our clients operating in Eastern Canada.

Our comprehensive service includes continuous disclosure filing; prospectus filings; additions to previous filings; set-up and amendments to SEDAR profiles; all other filings.
